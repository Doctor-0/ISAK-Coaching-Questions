Leadership Assessment

Aware: Deeply observe oneself and the environment, and understands what is important for self and the greater good
Connecting: Empathises with members of a community, understands their diverse potentials and values, and supports them towards a common goal
Creative: Generates innovative ideas and questions and builds upon those of others
Action-Taking: Steps out of one's comfort zone to courageously bring ideas to life
Self-Disciplined: Responds positvively to challenges with measured reflection, growth mindset, resilience and optimism
