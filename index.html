LO6. Engaged with issues of global importance
You should:
  • recognize the global implications of local issues
  • be able to identify global issues in the local or national community
  • show awareness of issues of global importance and takes concrete and appropriate actions in response to them either locally, nationally or internationally
  • get involved in CAS projects addressing global issues in a local, national or international context
  • develop awareness and responsibility towards a shared humanity

LO7. Considered the ethical implications of your choices and actions
You should:
  • recognizes ethical issues
  • be able to explain the social influences on one’s ethical identity
  • take into account cultural context when making a plan or ethical decision
  • identify what is needed to know in order to make an ethical decision
  • articulate ethical principles and approaches to ethical decisions
  • show accountability for choices and actions
  • be aware of the consequences of choices and actions regarding self, others involved and the community
  • integrate the process of reflection when facing an ethical decision
  • show awareness of the potential and varied consequences of choices and actions in planning and carrying out CAS experiences

Leadership Assessment

  • What steps have you taken to develop awareness and understanding of a need of ​global importance​ in your Leadership Project?
  • What methods for need assessment worked? Why? What can you do differently or will you do in the future? Why?
  • What understanding of need(s) - both explicit and implicit did you discover as a result?
  • What were moments during the project when you and your team needed to consider ethics in your choices and actions, i.e. Taking into account the cultural context in which you are working?
  • What ethical principles and/or values guided you and your teammates’ choices? Why were they important?
  • What are examples of successful outcomes that came from careful ethical decision making? What did not work as well? What can be done differently?
  • How has your team distributed roles and responsibilities in your team and why?
  • What successes and challenges have you encountered as a result of working in a team? How has / does your team intend to work intentionally with the challenges?
  • What mechanisms has your team put in place to maximize the strength of diversity in your team? What has worked well? What can be improved?
  • What mechanism have you built to support each other in the team? What has worked well? What can be improved?
  • How effectively has your team developed an action plan to carry out and develop your project over time?
  • How effectively has your team utilized the design thinking process to identify needs, come up with different solutions for your project, selected several solutions, prototyped them and improved them with feedback?
  • What worked well?
  • What didn’t work so well?
  • How can it be done differently?
  • How do you intend to stretch yourself and step out of your comfort zone through the leadership project?
  • What expected and unexpected challenges have you encountered personally and as a team? What thoughts and emotions arose as a result? How did you respond to these challenges intentionally?
  • How effectively has your team demonstrated perseverance and commitment to your leadership project activities?
  • What were moments in which you and/or your teammates didn’t respond effectively to challenges? What were the consequences? What can be done differently in the future?
  • How have you personally grown (skills/knowledge/mindsets) as a result of working with these challenges?
  • How has your team grown as a result of working with these challenges?
  • What are areas of your strengths that you wish to build on through the Leadership Project? Why is this important? How do you commit to growing these areas? How will you know if are growing in these strengths?
  • What are areas of your weaknesses that you wish to build on through the Leadership Project? Why is this important? How do you commit to growing these areas? How will you know if are growing in these strengths?
